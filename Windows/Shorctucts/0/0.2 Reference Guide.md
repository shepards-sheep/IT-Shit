## **4. Learning Processes ðŸ“š**

_The way people learn is shaped by different processes. Understanding these helps create effective learning environments._

There are three major learning theories:

- **Constructivism** â€“ Learners actively build knowledge from experience.
    
- **Behaviorism** â€“ Learning is reinforced through rewards and repetition.
    
- **Social Learning** â€“ Knowledge is gained through observing others.
    

---
